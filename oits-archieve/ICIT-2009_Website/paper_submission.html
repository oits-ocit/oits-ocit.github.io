PAPER SUBMISSION:

Manuscripts reporting high quality original and unpublished research are solicited for this conference. Single-spaced, double-column, 6-page manuscripts need to be formatted as per the  guidelines below. Manuscripts longer than 6 pages or disclosing the identity of the authors will not be reviewed.  The manuscripts will undergo double-blind review by a strong team of reviewers and program committee members consisting of leading researchers around the globe. All accepted papers will appear in the conference proceedings provided at least one of the authors registers for the conference. Tutorial presenters are requested to contact the tutorial chairs for the suitability of the topics and then submit a description of at most two pages along with the curriculum vitae of the proposers to one of the tutorial chairs. The manuscripts need to be submitted using the following online submission system.  The author can select appropriate track based on the description given below.

ICIT 2009 – Tracks and Topics

(For additional topics authors are advised to contact the associated track chair)

 

Architecture

Processor, cache, memory, and storage architectures, Multi-core computer systems, System integration/collaboration for systems of embedded systems, System-on-chip designs, HW/SW co-designs, Embedded system design practices, Networks-on-chip designs, Embedded system architectures, Design optimization (memory, performance etc.), Real-world applications and case studies, Power and energy-efficient architectures.

 

Computer Security

Access control in computer systems and networks, Authentication and authorization, Vulnerabilities, threat analysis, and risk assessment of systems and networks, Security models and methods, Security policies and mechanisms, Intrusion detection and incident response, Audit and accountability in computer systems, Network and communication security, Security in mobile and wireless networks, Security in ubiquitous, pervasive systems, Cryptography and security protocols, Key management in computer systems and networks, Survivable computer systems and networks, Reliable and fault-tolerance computer systems and networks, Trusted computing, Database security, Privacy protection and enhancement, Computer and network forensics, Security architecture and design, Information assurance, Information flow security.

 

Database and Bio-informatics

Distributed, parallel, grid-based, mobile, multimedia, spatial, temporal, geographic, statistical, and scientific databases,  Biological and medical  databases, bioinformatics, and computational biology,  Text databases and information retrieval, Machine learning and pattern recognition, Data mining and knowledge discovery.

 

Formal Methods and Logic

Automata and Logics, Formal Methods, and Formal Languages.

 

Learning Contents Management Systems and Education Technologies

Management Information Systems and Business Technologies (MIS & BT)

Accounting Information Systems, Analytical Modeling and Simulation, Data and  Information Quality, Decision Support, Knowledge and Data Management Systems, Diffusion of IT, e-Government, e-Commerce and e-Business, Emerging Information Technologies, Emerging Issues in IS Research, End-user Information Systems, Enterprise Systems, Gender Issues in IT, ICTs in Global Development, ICT in SMEs, Intelligent Systems, IS in Education, IS Outsourcing, IT in Health Care, IT Innovation, Social issues in IT.

 

Networks and Web Services
